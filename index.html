Rules of the Realm

Spend under budget and the difference becomes gold.

Hit the budget exactly and you survive the day. No glory, no shame, no parade.

Overspend and your streak breaks. Your kingdom will continue existing, but with disappointment.

As your gold grows, your rank rises and your village unlocks peasants, farms, mills, barracks, walls, knights, and eventually an empire.

In other words: discipline builds civilization.
