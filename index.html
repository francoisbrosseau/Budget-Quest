Budget Quest

Build your kingdom by spending below your own limit. Set your daily budget, report what you actually spent, choose your currency, earn controlled gold at a balanced rate, unlock villagers and structures, and climb from peon to emperor. Overspend if you want. The realm will not stop you. It will simply remember.

Log Your Day

Set your ruler, commit to a budget, and close the day. Your savings now convert to gold at a reduced balanced rate, with extra passive income from unlocked villagers and buildings.
Gold is the reward currency no matter which real-world currency you track in.

Rules of the Realm

Saved money no longer converts 1:1 into gold. That was way too generous and frankly irresponsible.

New formula: roughly 1 gold per 5 units saved, with a minimum of 1 gold when you finish under budget.

Unlocks are now much more expensive, and some of them generate passive gold every successful day.

Exact budget counts as survival, not glory. Overspending breaks the streak and earns nothing.

Rank upgrades improve your visual character from tavern drifter to imperial ruler.
