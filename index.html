Rules of the Realm

Saved money no longer converts 1:1 into gold. That was way too generous and frankly irresponsible.

New formula: roughly 1 gold per 5 units saved, with a minimum of 1 gold when you finish under budget.

Unlocks are now much more expensive, and some of them generate passive gold every successful day.

Exact budget counts as survival, not glory. Overspending breaks the streak and earns nothing.

Portrait assets now follow this naming rule:

assets/rulers/GENDER/GENDER_HAIRCUT_COLOR_BEARD.png

Example:
assets/rulers/male/male_1_black_clean.png

So later you can add male/female, haircut 1/2/3, clean/beard, and many colors without rewriting the logic.
